Coconut Rice

Description

coconut rice is made with coconut flakes (or grated or desiccated/dry coconut). One way to make this dish is to make the rice separately (preferably using a rice variety which is light and fluffy when cooked) and then mixing it with the coconut mixture (coconut flakes toasted in sesame/coconut oil and spiced with paprika, nuts, curry powder/leaves and other spices).

Ingredients

  • 5-7 cups of cooked, cooled white long grain rice(light and fluffy)
  • 0.5 cup of fresh/frozen grated coconut or dry coconut flakes
  • 2 dry red chilies
  • 1 teaspoon black mustard seed
  • 7-10 curry leaves
  • 1 teaspoon Chana dhal
  • 2 teaspoon oil
  • salt to taste
  • a few cashews

Steps

  1. Wash the rice and cook it (either in rice cooker or over the stove) until light and fluffy.
  2. Let it cool and firm up a bit. May also use leftover white rice from previous day.
  3. Heat the oil with the mustard seeds in a covered saute pan.
  4. When the mustard seeds have finished popping, add the other ingredients and lightly toast them in the oil till they change to a golden color.
  5. Then add the seasonings to cooked rice, mix well and serve.
